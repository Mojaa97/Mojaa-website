For AY 2026-27, the ITR filing deadlines are: ITR-1 and ITR-2 by 31 July 2026, ITR-3 and ITR-4 by 31 August 2026, tax audit cases by 31 October 2026, and transfer pricing cases by 30 November 2026.

## Which ITR Form Should You File?

ITR-1 is for salaried individuals with income up to Rs 50 lakh. ITR-2 is for those with capital gains, foreign assets, or income above Rs 50 lakh. ITR-3 is for business and professional income including F&O trading. ITR-4 is for presumptive taxation under Section 44AD, 44ADA or 44AE. ITR-5 is for LLPs and firms. ITR-6 is for companies. ITR-7 is for trusts and institutions.

## Belated and Revised Returns

A belated return can be filed until 31 December 2026 under Section 139(4) with a fee of up to Rs 5,000 under Section 234F. A revised return can be filed until 31 March 2027 under Section 139(5).

## Does the New Income Tax Act 2025 Apply?

No. Your AY 2026-27 return is governed by the Income Tax Act 1961. The Income Tax Act 2025 applies from Tax Year 2026-27 onwards.
